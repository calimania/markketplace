# Integrating Stripe Connect for Payouts

We are excited to announce that our platform leverages Stripe Connect for
managing payouts. Stripe Connect is a comprehensive payment solution designed to
cater to your business needs, ensuring the highest standards of safety, privacy,
and efficiency.

## Why Stripe Connect?

Stripe Connect integrates directly with your bank, providing a seamless view of
transaction states. It also connects to your accounting software, making it
easier to manage your finances and customers. Here's why we chose Stripe
Connect:

- **Safety and Privacy**: Stripe's top-notch security measures ensure that your financial data is always protected.
- **Bank Integration**: Directly connect to your bank for a real-time view of your transactions.
- **Accounting Software Compatibility**: Easily sync with your accounting software to streamline your financial operations.
- **Customer and Order Management**: Manage your customers and orders effortlessly, all in one place.

## Using Stripe Embedded React Components

Our platform utilizes Stripe's embedded React components, allowing for a smooth
and secure payment experience. These components are integrated with our API keys
to ensure secure transactions.

### For Self-Hosted Users

If you are using a self-hosted version of our platform, you will need to create your own Stripe Connect accounts. This is a simple process that allows you to customize your payment flow and maintain control over your payouts.

## Future Updates

We are continuously working to enhance our platform. Future updates will include
additional features for payment customization, giving you even more control over
how you manage and receive payments.
